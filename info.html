Graph search for prog music


The Project


ProgToNode is a new way to explore the world of progressive music. With ProgToNode you'll be able to search any artist, band or project of progressive rock/metal and discover the relationships between them.

The cool thing is that this relationships are represented in a visual way implementing graphs theory.

Yes, we put together prog music and math. Isn't that cool?


Why ProgToNode

We are building a graphic encyclopedia of progressive music

It's Easy


Reading lines and nodes connecting musicians and bands is a cool way of learning and discovering new music. The posiblities are endless.

It's Fun


You can spend hours connecting artists, you can even get surprised to where you get.

It's Experimental


The progressive music is about experimenting and reaching unknown horizons. By implementing mathematical methods, graphs theory and the hypothesis of six degrees of separation, we are looking for expanding these horizons of discovery.
