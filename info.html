Graph search for prog music


The Project


ProgToNode is a new way to explore the world of progressive music. With ProgToNode you'll be able to search any artist, band or project of progressive rock/metal and discover the relationships between them.

The cool thing is that this relationships are represented in a visual way implementing graphs theory.

Yes, we put together prog music and math. Isn't that cool?


Why ProgToNode

We are building a graphic encyclopedia of progressive music

It's Easy


Reading lines and nodes connecting musicians and bands is a cool way of learning and discovering new music. The posiblities are endless.

It's Fun


You can spend hours connecting artists, you can even get surprised to where you get.

It's Experimental


The progressive music is about experimenting and reaching unknown horizons. By implementing mathematical methods, graphs theory and the hypothesis of six degrees of separation, we are looking for expanding these horizons of discovery.

Features




Graph Searching

Do you want to see every project or members of your favorite prog rock band/musician?. Type the name in the search box and you will see the nodes linking each other in seconds.

You are able to expand this graph by setting the controls, zoom in/out, and view it full screen.



Connecting Artists

You can connect the different artists and begin a journey through the universe of prog music. You can get very suprised of what you find (it happened to us). Begin by doing click in any node of your graph, you'll see a new graph with this artist/band.

If you wanna see your journey in steps, click in Degrees button.



Listening playlists and discovering albums

This experience would be pointless without listening some good prog music. Make click in Videos button and listen a playlist from Youtube of your artist.

Close the window and you can hear the music while navigating. Also you can see the dischography of your current artist



Search modes

You have two modes of searching. Planet Mode will show you results of artists and bands. System Mode will show you results of albums, you are able to build a graph with every musician participating in the record.

Yes, we know that prog nerds are obsessed with albums
